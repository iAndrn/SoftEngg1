Internet and Printing

Pureza/Anonas Area

Pureza Street is known as a passageway into the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. The PUP Arc is shown before the I-tech building (Institute of Technology). Various Internet Cafe's and Printing Services are scattered so students spend their time playing, doing their assignments or searching for the cheapest printing service for schoolworks and papers.

Popular and Trending Internet Cafes in Pureza Area

Here is the list of Internet Cafes in Pureza:

Around Pureza/College of Engineering and Architecture

  • 263 CyberCafe
  • Infinity Internet Cafe
  • Orb Internet Cafe
  • D'rat Internet Cafe

263 CyberCafe

263 Internet Cafe is one of the highest quality Internet Cafe in the entire PUP Compound (Unbiased Opinion though). Featuring its high end computers with different specifications suited for different types of players and users. They also offer printing services which is more pricey than competitors. While gaming, you can also buy foods from the shop inside the cafe.
