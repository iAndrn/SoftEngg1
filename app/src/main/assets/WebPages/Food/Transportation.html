How to Get   Here

LRT Trains

Most commonly used modes of transportation to get to PUP is the Light Rail Transit or LRT. Connecting the long distance places is the main reason mass public uses the LRT and also as PUPian the use of the LRT is as common as riding the Jeepney. Students from Rizal and Antipolo are the ones that commonly uses this mode of transportation. Just outside the Pureza station pedicabs are parked and ready to take you to the Main Campus

PNR Trains

Aside from the LRT Trains another rail transit is also present in PUP and this one is much closer to the Campus literally close because before you cross the gates of PUP you must first cross the rails of PNR. PNR connects the people of south to the Campus the pros of riding the train is its speed but it also has its cons due to it being a public mass transportation, if you do not manage your time well you may end up in a jammed pack train full of variety of commuters. Another mode of transportation that uses the rails are trolleys, Teresa houses many trolley drivers thus making it an indirect terminal for them, this maybe illegal but still its a faster way to go to the Campus. The Station is just few steps from the gates

PEDICABS and TRICYCLE

This is the only means of transportation the may go infront of your building maybe it inside the main campus or outside just kindly ask the driver to take you inside, no additional fare. The signature lines of drivers of tricycle in front of CEA and in Pureza "oh meyn meyn" meaning that its end route is in the Main Campus

FERRY

Roads and Streets aren't the only way to enter PUP, waterways are also utilize by PUPians. Since the MMDA started the ferry system in Pasig River one of the Station PUP since the campus is situated right beside the river. Its a fast way to go to PUP from Pasig without the hassle of road traffic. The Station is just beside the Linear Park
